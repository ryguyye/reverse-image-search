selfwatch

Upload your own photo (or paste a URL to one). The app queries reverse image search providers and shows where copies of that image appear online — useful for impersonation, scraping, or copyright monitoring.

Uploads are saved to ./uploads and served at /uploads/<name>. The app must be reachable from the public internet for SerpAPI to fetch them — easiest path is make tunnel (Cloudflare). See docs/deploy.md.

Watches

Save an image to be re-scanned periodically. When new matches appear, optionally POST them to a webhook (Slack, Discord, Zapier, your own endpoint).

Uploads require PUBLIC_BASE_URL in .env for the scheduler to fetch them.
